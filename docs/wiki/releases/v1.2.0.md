# RunTiyul v1.2.0

Release date: 2026-07-18<br>
App version: `1.2.0+5`

RunTiyul v1.2.0 adds eyes-free navigation feedback and fixes two route-map
problems reported during activity testing.

## Highlights

### Navigation alerts

- Added configurable **Tone + voice**, **Voice**, **Tones**, and **Haptics only**
  output for persistent off-route and upcoming-junction alerts.
- Added concise offline system-voice prompts such as “Off route. Check the map”
  and “In 25 meters, keep left.”
- Voice mode selects an installed non-network English voice on Android and
  falls back to the corresponding bundled tone when speech is unavailable.
- Added separate **Off route** and **Junction** preview buttons under
  **Record → Alerts**. Previews use the currently displayed settings before
  they are saved.
- Added navigation audio focus, music ducking, Bluetooth-aware iOS voice-prompt
  configuration, and best-effort playback that cannot interrupt GPS recording.

### Route-map fixes

- Route detail and waypoint-editor bottom panels now honor the device safe area
  instead of being covered by edge-to-edge system navigation.
- Realtime activity maps now receive all saved routes when the saved-trails
  layer is enabled.
- The selected navigation route remains visible when additional saved routes
  are hidden.

## Audio licensing

The two bundled navigation cues come from Kenney **Interface Sounds 1.0** under
CC0 1.0 Universal. Source names, SHA-256 hashes, and provenance are retained in
`assets/audio/navigation/LICENSE.txt`.

## Known limitations

- Navigation alert audibility, Bluetooth/open-ear routing, and locked-screen
  playback still require physical-device testing outdoors on Android and iOS.
- Voice prompts are basic route-status/junction guidance, not full turn-by-turn
  navigation.
- Route progress percentage and course-up mode remain unimplemented.
- iOS runtime and sideload behavior remain unverified despite CI producing an
  unsigned IPA.
- Production offline-map hosting/provider approval and release signing remain
  unresolved.
- `flutter_tts` 4.2.5 emits a non-blocking warning about Flutter's future
  Built-in Kotlin migration.
